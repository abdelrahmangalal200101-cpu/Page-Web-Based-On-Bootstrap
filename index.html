Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and
investing in real estate

Current mortgage rates vary depending on the loan type and your credit score. For a 30-year fixed mortgage, rates typically range from 6.5% to 7.2%, while 15-year fixed mortgages are usually 0.5-0.75% lower. Our financial advisors can help you secure the best rates based on your specific situation and connect you with trusted lenders in our network.

While the traditional recommendation is 20% to avoid PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance), many buyers successfully purchase homes with as little as 3-5% down. For example, on a $400,000 home, that's $12,000-$20,000 instead of $80,000. We also recommend setting aside an additional 2-3% of the purchase price for closing costs and initial home expenses.

A listing agent represents the seller and works to get them the highest possible price, while a buyer's agent represents you and negotiates on your behalf to secure the best deal. At EliteHomes, our buyer's agents are experienced negotiators who know the local market inside and out. Best of all, the buyer's agent commission is typically paid by the seller, so you get expert representation at no direct cost to you.

The typical closing timeline is 30-45 days from when your offer is accepted. This includes time for home inspections, appraisals, mortgage underwriting, and final paperwork. However, cash buyers can often close in as little as 7-14 days. Our team coordinates every step of the process to ensure a smooth and timely closing, keeping you informed throughout.

Absolutely! A professional home inspection is one of the most important steps in the buying process. It typically costs $300-$500 but can save you thousands by identifying issues like foundation problems, roof damage, electrical issues, or plumbing concerns before you commit. We work with certified inspectors who provide detailed reports, and we'll help you negotiate repairs or price adjustments based on their findings.

Real estate has historically been one of the most reliable long-term investments. While market conditions fluctuate, property values have consistently appreciated over time, averaging 3-5% annually nationwide. With current inventory levels and strategic buying opportunities, many investors are finding excellent deals in emerging markets. Our investment specialists can analyze your goals and identify properties with the strongest potential for appreciation and rental income.

Closing costs typically range from 2-5% of the home's purchase price and include fees for title insurance, attorney services, loan origination, appraisals, and recording fees. While buyers usually pay the majority of these costs, they're often negotiable. In competitive markets, sellers may agree to cover some or all closing costs to close the deal. Our agents are skilled negotiators who will work to minimize your out-of-pocket expenses at closing.
